About Us

At Lane 4 Less Development Inc., we're experts in optimizing urban and rural spaces by crafting unique living solutions like laneway houses, garden suites, and charming cottages. Our mission is to enhance your property's value and functionality with beautiful, sustainable, and efficient homes.

Our Services

Laneway Houses

These are smart, compact homes built on the existing lots of residential properties, usually in the backyard and accessible by an alley or lane. Ideal for urban environments, laneway houses are perfect as secondary rental units or extended family living spaces, providing privacy and convenience without the cost of purchasing new land.

Garden Suites

Similar to laneway houses but located within the green spaces of your property, garden suites offer a tranquil retreat. These detached units serve as excellent options for guest houses or rental properties, integrating seamlessly with your garden's landscape to create a serene living area.

Cottages

Our custom-built cottages are designed to provide a cozy, rustic escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Perfect for rural settings or as a vacation getaway, these charming structures are crafted with aesthetics and functionality in mind, offering a peaceful living solution wherever you choose to build.
